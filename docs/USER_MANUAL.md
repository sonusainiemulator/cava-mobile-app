## Step-by-Step Guides

### 📷 How to Scan a Product

1. **Open the Scanner**
   - From home screen, tap the **SCAN** button
   
2. **Point at Barcode**
   - Position your camera so the barcode fills the frame
   - Hold steady until scan completes
   
3. **View Product Info**
   - A dialog appears with product details:
     - Name
     - Brand
     - Presentation (bottle size)
     - Alcohol content
     - Price
   
4. **Add to Your Cava** (Optional)
   - Tap **Add to Cava** button
   - Product is saved to your collection
   
5. **Continue Scanning**
   - Tap **Cancel** to scan another product

**Pro Tip**: Make sure you have good lighting for best results!

### ⭐ Writing a Review

1. **Open Reviews**
   - Tap **REVIEWS** from home screen
   
2. **Read Existing Reviews**
   - Scroll through community reviews
   - See ratings (1-5 stars) and comments
   
3. **Add Your Review**
   - Tap the **+** (plus) button
   
4. **Fill Out the Form**
   - **Username**: Your display name
   - **Comment**: Your tasting notes (be descriptive!)
   - **Rating**: Slide to select 1-5 stars
   
5. **Submit**
   - Tap **Submit** button
   - Your review will appear after approval

**Review Tips**:
- Describe the aroma, taste, and finish
- Mention food pairings
- Be honest and helpful
- Respectful reviews only!

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Scanner Not Working
**Problem**: Camera won't scan barcodes
**Solutions**:
1. Check camera permissions in device settings
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Clean camera lens
4. Hold phone steady
5. Make sure barcode is in focus

#### Reviews Not Appearing
**Problem**: My review doesn't show up
**Solution**: Reviews must be approved before appearing publicly. This usually takes 24-48 hours.

#### Can't Add to Cava
**Problem**: Items won't add to collection
**Solutions**:
1. Check if item is already in your Cava
2. Try restarting the app
3. Clear app cache

#### Location Not Working
**Problem**: Events not showing
**Solutions**:
1. Enable location services
2. Grant location permission to app
3. Check GPS is working on device

#### Chat Messages Not Sending
**Problem**: Messages stuck
**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Restart the app
3. Verify recipient username is correct

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is the app free?**
A: Yes! The base app is completely free to use.

**Q: Do I need internet?**
A: Most features work offline. Syncing, events, and chat require internet.

**Q: Can I use this in a store?**
A: Yes! Scan products while shopping to learn more about them.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. Your collection and scans are stored locally on your device.

### Features

**Q: How many items can I add to my Cava?**
A: Unlimited! Add as many as you like.

**Q: Can I edit reviews after posting?**
A: Not yet, but this feature is coming soon!

**Q: Can I delete items from my Cava?**
A: This feature is coming in the next update.

**Q: Are the prices in the app accurate?**
A: Prices are estimates and may vary by location and retailer.

### Technical

**Q: What devices are supported?**
A: Android 5.0+ and iOS 12.0+

**Q: How much storage does the app use?**
A: Approximately 50-100 MB depending on your collection size.

**Q: Can I export my collection?**
A: Export feature coming soon!

**Q: Is there a web version?**
A: Not yet, mobile only for now.
